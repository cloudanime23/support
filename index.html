Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I create an account?
  • How can I reset my password?
  • How do I connect with other users?

How do I create an account?

To create an account, click on the "Sign Up" button on the home page and fill in the required information.

How can I reset my password?

If you need to reset your password, click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page and follow the instructions.

How do I connect with other users?

You can connect with other users by visiting the "Community" section of the app and sending them a friend request.

Support Hours
